About Jhansi

Jhansi is a historic city in Uttar Pradesh, India. It is famous for Rani Lakshmi Bai and its important role in the Revolt of 1857. Jhansi is a city of bravery, culture, and pride.

Major Dhyan Chand

Major Dhyan Chand, known as the "Wizard of Hockey", was born in Allahabad but had strong connections with Jhansi where he grew up and practiced hockey. He was one of the greatest hockey players in the world.

  • Won 3 Olympic Gold Medals (1928, 1932, 1936)
  • Known for extraordinary ball control
  • National Sports Day in India is celebrated on 29 August (his birthday)
